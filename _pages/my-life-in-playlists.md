_Each is a personal time machine._

I've kept an autobiographical playlist since 2008. This was inspired by Thom Yorke's practice of posting playlists on W.A.S.T.E. HQ every now and then. I loved how it was just text and it was up to you to to go and find the songs if you wanted to listen. I did the same, but then streaming happened so playslists from 2016 onward are (mostly) available on Spotify.
