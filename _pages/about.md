I was born and raised in the American midwest but now live in California with [Sage](https://etters.co). Her and I have been sharing our lives together since 2010. I'm often [reading](/books) fiction, theory, and science. I love travel, the smell of fresh acrylic paint, and to be on the stage.

My practice incorporates software, electronic music, writing, new media, design, photography, community organizing, and teaching. I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts in graphic design from Columbia College Chicago.
