- Making post-pandemic future music as [Sidereal Lobby](https://sidereallobby.com) and [STUXNET](https://stuxnet.me).
- Organizing [Flash Crash](https://flashcrash.net).
- Teaching and sharing at [Map Corps](https://mapcorps.net) and the [monome community](https://llllllll.co).
- Serving as Vice President at [Highland](https://highlandsolutions.com).
- Thinking about the future a lot.
