[Intertext](https://intertext.bandcamp.com) is where I release my music. It is a hermitic, one-man, non-label. Intertext has no branding, no lettermark, no mission, no social media presence, no marketing strategy. This paragraph is the only "about" section that exists. Mark Fisher introduced me to the word and if you're looking to get lost in a wormhole, I highly recommend [this Wikipedia article](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intertextuality).
