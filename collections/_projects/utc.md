[Coordinated Universal Time](https://apodcastfornow.com) was a podcast for now. Conceived as a semiotic blitz of found sounds, field recordings, interviews, theory-crafting, science-fiction, sonic collage, and original dub techno. We only had 5 episodes but it was an incredible ride. The [first episode](https://anchor.fm/apodcastfornow/episodes/To-Realize-a-Commoner-Earth-e43vu5) will go down in the history books as one of my favorite pieces of work.
