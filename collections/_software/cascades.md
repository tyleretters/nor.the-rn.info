Cascades is a grid and arc application for Max4Live. It is the successor to Cascade. It lets you paint with all 65,536 combinations of 16th note patterns across seven tracks. See the thread [over at llllllll.co](http://l.llllllll.co/cascades) and watch [the demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zTj_hnY7NlM).
