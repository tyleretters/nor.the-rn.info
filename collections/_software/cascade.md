Cascade is a M4L sequencer that holds every combination of eighth notes possible in a 4/4 bar. I wrote a [lengthly post about it on Highland's blog](https://journal.highlandsolutions.com/meet-cascade-a-piece-of-music-software-i-built-with-max-and-rapid-prototyping-d8ce9bda3a5b) and made [a short demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VQhQaYClE78&feature=youtu.be).

[Download the patch, M4L Device, and demo.](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/northerninformation/cascade.zip)
