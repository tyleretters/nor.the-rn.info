Markov Music is a grid application for Max. It uses a simple three-node Markov chain as a backbone to let you create melodies and rhythms. See the thread [over at llllllll.co](http://l.llllllll.co/markov) and watch [the demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ta6-JZP_aJ0).
