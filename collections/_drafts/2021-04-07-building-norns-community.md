This is a visual account of building [https://norns.community](https://norns.community). It is divded into 4 sections:

1. Overview
2. The Design Challenge

4. Conclusion

Most of the work was done in the month of March. Principal contributors were myself, [@eigen](https://norns.community/en/authors/eigen), and [@dan_derks](https://norns.community/en/authors/dan_derks). (_Meta: dang, that feels good to link to this site!_) As usual, this project saw plenty of collaboration from dozens of members of the monome community.

I'm really proud of the work we did, how quickly we did it, and how we worked together.
