## Prologue

This is a story that has been written a million times, but it needs to be written again. Because we artists always forget about it. And because you came here to read something.

It is a story of the ecstatic highs and the soul crushing lows that accompany any "serious" art-making. Yes, dear reader, I fear I am (and have always been) one of those artists who takes their work perhaps a little too seriously. Puts too much of themselves into it, you know? Can't just sit down and "mess around" with my work for a bit. Be like the legions of idealized imaginary artists who live down the street.

They know how to have fun with their art. They take it lightly. It is a joy, always. Never a care in the world. No dreams about it. No *mindflares* (my name for when thoughts get caught in a whirlwind of creation and literally nothing I do will stop them.) Spiraling microbursts of nervous system impulses, churning geodesic synapse fractal, unbounded creation. The only way out is through. Through your medium.

Today's medium is podcast.

## A Podcast for Now

As I type, episode one of [Coordinated Universal Time](https://apodcastfornow.com) is merrily propagating its little way through the mycelium of the internet. We uploaded it to [Anchor.fm](https://anchor.fm) this morning.

I've been doing this type of thing long enough that I had the foresight to note down the date and time when the words first materialized. On April 3rd, 2019 I shared an idea in the Locutory:

![UTC Origin](/assets/images/utc-origin.png)

Four days later we had a 10 minute demo completed. There were only three segments. [@argotechnica](https://twitter.com/argotechnica), [@notinferno](https://twitter.com/ntnfrno), and I each read some words into our field recorders. A fresh dub-techno track that [@MatthewTheStoat](https://twitter.com/MatthewTheStoat) and I worked on the previous day became the intro background music. I mixed it in under an hour.

Dropbox.

We sent the demo to our trusted friends and confidants.

Responses in the positive.

Curiousity.

Encouragement.

What they heck? Did we have a live one?

## Coordinated Universal Time

So what's the story that has been told a million times? It is the story of communal labor and ideals. The story of a bunch of people coming together and creating something awesome and maybe learning a thing or two about themselves, each other, and the world along the way. At this point I personally don't care if no one listens. We made it for us. We made it for you.

So maybe, just maybe - in a really beautiful and roundabout way - I've suddenly become that idealized artist down the street who was just messing around.
